SERVICES

Liquid Geomatics provides consultancy services in the fields of geospatial data acquisition and analysis. Common services include land and water mapping projects, research, algorithm and software development, and training.

We have completed a wide range of projects for Canadian and foreign organizations, including terrestrial and marine mapping projects employing the full range of remote sensing technologies (UAVs, manned aircraft, satellites).

Our clients primarily include governmental and non-governmental entities. We collaborate on our projects with governmental, academic and private-sector partners to deliver state-of-the-art solutions.
